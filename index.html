Practitioner Survey — ERP Audit Experience & GRC Utilisation

Purpose of This Survey

This survey supports a dissertation titled "Audit of ERP Systems in Public Sector Undertakings", conducted by Imsurenla Longkumer, IA&AS Officer Trainee, NAAA. It examines how well ERP systems in Indian PSUs are being used to enforce financial controls, and whether CAG audit teams have the tools and skills to audit those controls effectively.

All responses are confidential and reported only in aggregate. The survey takes approximately 8–10 minutes.

Instructions: For each statement in Sections B–E, tick the number that best matches your experience. If you have limited direct experience of a particular area, answer based on your general professional knowledge and observation. If you genuinely have no basis to answer a question at all, leave it blank. For open-text questions, even one sentence is helpful. You do not need to know technical SAP terminology to answer — answer from what you have seen or experienced.
